Come Visit Broward Mall in Plantation, Miami FL: Hours and Holiday Schedule

If you’re looking to get some shopping done or just spend some time browsing the latest trends, Broward Mall in Plantation, Miami FL, is the perfect destination. With a vast range of stores and amenities, there is something for every age group and interest. However, before you visit, it’s essential to know the store’s hours so that you can make the most of your trip. In this article, we’ll cover Broward Mall’s operating hours and holiday schedule so you can make informed decisions.

What Time Does Broward Mall Open and Close?

Broward Mall operates from 10 am to 9 pm from Monday to Saturday, and 11 am to 7 pm on Sundays. These hours apply to both the mall and most of its stores. The only exception is on Sunday when some of the mall’s stores open at noon.

Is Broward Mall Open on Saturday and Sunday?

Yes, Broward Mall is open on both Saturdays and Sundays. As mentioned above, the mall operates from 10 am to 9 pm on Saturdays and 11 am to 7 pm on Sundays. So, weekends are perfect for shopping or enjoying some of the mall’s amenities.

Regular Operating Hours

Here’s a rundown of the store’s regular operating hours:

  • Monday: 10 am – 9 pm
  • Tuesday: 10 am – 9 pm
  • Wednesday: 10 am – 9 pm
  • Thursday: 10 am – 9 pm
  • Friday: 10 am – 9 pm
  • Saturday: 10 am – 9 pm
  • Sunday: 11 am – 7 pm (some stores open at noon)

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you make the most of your visit to Broward Mall:

  • It’s always best to check the mall’s hours before you visit to avoid any surprises, especially on holidays.
  • Most stores typically open at the same time as the mall, but it’s advisable to double-check with individual stores if you’re unsure.
  • Weekdays are generally less busy than weekends, so if you want a quieter, hassle-free shopping experience, aim to visit on a weekday.
  • If you’re visiting on a holiday, plan your visit around the adjusted operating hours so you don’t miss out on anything.
  • Broward Mall typically has extended hours during the holiday season, so if you’re looking for a late-night shopping binge, keep that in mind.

Conclusion

Broward Mall in Plantation, Miami FL, is a fantastic destination for doing some shopping or simply hanging out and enjoying the latest trends. In this article, we’ve covered the regular operating hours and holiday schedule, as well as some tips and tricks to help you have a great shopping experience. So, plan ahead, arrive early and shop to your heart’s content!
