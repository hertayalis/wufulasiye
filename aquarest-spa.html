What is the Aquarest Spa?

The Aquarest Spa is an easy-to-use hot tub that doesn’t skimp on the bells and whistles—even those not usually seen in hot tubs that retail for under $10,000. A stone facade mimics natural hot springs, offering an organic look. Snag it in three neutral colors: graystone, brownstone and keystone look pretty sitting on a porch or patio.

An ozone water purification system keeps bacteria at bay, cutting out nearly 75% of the chemicals normally seen in home jacuzzis. It’s the perfect choice for families. All you need is an outdoor privacy screen or patio shade sail, and your personal backyard spa awaits. Enhance your experience with these backyard hot tub privacy ideas.

Sit back and relax in one of four captain’s seats, equipped with ample lumbar arch support and personal jets that soothe and eliminate stress. An LED-backlit waterfall, nine LED color settings and built-in cupholders create a soothing experience. Say “no” to burnout—it’s the ideal way to unwind after work.

How to Drain the Aquarest Spa

The spa is simple to drain. Turn off the power, then remove the drain valve cap and move it to the open position. Then, connect a hose to the nozzle of the valve to direct water away from your backyard and home. Once fully drained, it’s beneficial to flip the hot tub upside down to ensure all water drains out. This is especially important to effectively winterize your hot tub.
