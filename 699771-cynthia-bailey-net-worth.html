Cynthia Bailey Net Worth 2023: What Is The RHOA Star Worth?

Cynthia Bailey, a name that resonates with fans of The Real Housewives of Atlanta (RHOA), has significantly impacted the entertainment world. Born on February 19, 1967, in Decatur, Alabama, Bailey's journey to stardom began at a young age. Her talent was recognized when she was crowned the first Black homecoming queen at Deshler High School. This achievement led to her being scouted by a talent scout for Wilhelmina Models, marking the start of her modeling career.

Bailey moved to New York City at 18, where she signed a five-year contract with Wilhelmina Models. Her career took off, and she soon graced the covers of renowned magazines and walked the runway in fashion shows in Paris and Milan. Her first significant booking was Essence Magazine's cover, followed by modeling jobs for Target, Maybelline, and Oil of Olay. She also appeared in Vogue, Vanity Fair, Elle, and Glamour magazine spreads.

The RHOA Star

NEW YORK, NEW YORK - OCTOBER 14: Cynthia Bailey attends the Legends Ball during 2022 BravoCon. At Manhattan Center on October 14, 2022 in New York City. (Photo by Santiago Felipe/Getty Images)

In 2010, Bailey joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Atlanta, which significantly boosted her fame and fortune. Initially hesitant about joining the popular Bravo franchise, Bailey eventually saw it as a business opportunity. The show focused on her relationship with nightclub owner Peter Thomas and her friendship with NeNe Leakes, providing viewers with an intimate look into her life.

Beyond her modeling and reality TV career, Bailey has proven to be a savvy businesswoman. In 2011, she opened a modeling school in Atlanta, and in 2019, she launched the Bailey Room Wine Cellar, an event space in Atlanta. Despite the challenges posed by the Covid-19 pandemic, Bailey reopened her wine cellar, demonstrating her resilience and business acumen.

Personal Life & Achievements

Bailey's personal life has also been a topic of interest for her fans. She has a daughter, Noelle, with actor and singer Leon Robinson, whom she considers her greatest personal accomplishment. Bailey was also married to nightclub impresario Peter Thomas from 2010 to 2017. In 2018, she publicly announced her relationship with Fox Sports correspondent Mike Hill, with the couple getting engaged in 2019. They, too, recently decided to end their marriage.

As of 2023, Cynthia Bailey's net worth is estimated to be around $2.5 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. This wealth is a testament to her successful career in modeling, her role in The Real Housewives of Atlanta, and her various business ventures. Bailey's story inspires many, showing that one can achieve great success with hard work, ambition, and faith.
