Jack Draper career

Draper, who has struggled with injuries for the whole of his career, made his ATP Tour main draw debut in singles at the Miami Open as a wildcard.

For the first time in his career, he advanced to the quarterfinals of an ATP competition where he lost to eventual champion Cameron Norrie after defeating world No. 39 Alexander Bublik in the round of 16.

At the Wimbledon Championships, he was given a wildcard into the singles main tournament. In the first round, he faced up against reigning champion Novak Djokovic, who he defeated in three sets after taking the opening set 6-4.

Draper joined the ATP Challenger Tour event, the 2022 Città di Forl II, in Forl in January as the eighth seed. There, he defeated fellow countryman Jay Clarke, 6-3, 6-0, to go to his maiden Challenger final and win his first championship on the tour.
