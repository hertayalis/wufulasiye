Biography

Lee Tae-im was born in Ulsan, South Korea on September 2, 1986. Actor who rose to fame playing Jang Soo-Hyun on the Korean drama series Don’t Hesitate. She has since landed roles on shows like Woman of Dignity as well as in films like S.I.U. (2011) and For the Emperor (2014). She had a highly-publicized conflict with Kim Ye-won on the set of the show My Tutor Friend. She graduated from Sung-Gwang High School. She majored in theater and film at Hanyang University before dropping out to pursue acting.
