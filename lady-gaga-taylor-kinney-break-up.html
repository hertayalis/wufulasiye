Lady Gaga and Fianc Taylor Kinney Break Up

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Update, 7/20: After news of their split broke yesterday, Lady Gaga took to Instagram last night to open up about what happened between her and her fiancé Taylor Kinney. The couple has been "taking a break," but they're working through their conflicting work schedules and long-distance relationships to "continue the simple love [they] have always shared," Gaga explained.

The singer shared a black-and-white photo of her and Kinney walking arm-in-arm, and wrote this in the caption:

"Taylor and I have always believed we are soulmates. Just like all couples we have ups and downs, and we have been taking a break. We are both ambitious artists, hoping to work through long-distance and complicated schedules to continue the simple love we have always shared. Please root us on. We're just like everybody else and we really love each other."

Original post, 7/19: Lady Gaga and fiancé Taylor Kinney have broken up after five years together, TMZ reports.The couple apparently split earlier this month—and Gaga is no longer wearing her engagement ring.

Kinney, 35, proposed to the singer, 30, on Valentine's Day in 2015. The pair never publicly announced a wedding date, but they did give the ceremony some thought—though they kept most details under wraps. "We'll keep it a surprise," Kinney said in January this year. "We are not planning anything that is worth writing about," Gaga told Ryan Seacrest in March. She was also planning on taking her fiancé's last name.

The couple first met on the set of Gaga's music video for "Yoü and I." Watch it and reminisce on their relationship below.
